## Description

```
CONTEXT
During the last quarter of 2022, Burger King® announced a collaboration with Call Of Duty Modern Warfare II. The game was one of the most awaited releases in the industry and, with the partnership, people were expecting great things.

IDEA
Based on the fact that Call Of Duty was promoted mostly as a night battle game, Burger King created an immersive offline experience available only in Puerto Rico that included an AR filter in which people could use their phones their phones to look through night vision goggles, a restaurant that looked like a CoD scenario, a limited edition combo, and a promotional campaign that put people, in the game characters' shoes.

EXECUTION
For one month, people were invited to be part of the full experience, using the filter to find clues hidden in the customized restaurants in order to win discounts, free limited edition combos. rewards on the BK® App, and even some game skins.
```
