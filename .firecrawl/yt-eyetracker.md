## Description

```
Our approach centered on transforming a historically ingrained behavior—the rush to overstock at the announcement of a storm—and positioning SuperMax as the singular supermarket on the island dedicated to offering a pragmatic solution to this challenge. We recognized the need for a shift towards collective responsibility and rational preparation in the face of impending storms. To achieve this, we created a proprietary technology designed to guide users towards adequately preparing and stocking up before a storm, thereby minimizing panic-induced chaos and preventing overstocking. This innovative solution not only facilitated a more orderly and stress-free preparation process for individuals but also ensured that essential supplies remained accessible for the entire community, allowing everyone the opportunity to prepare adequately. Our initiative was rooted in the belief that by fostering a more informed and calm approach to storm preparation, we could significantly contribute to a more resilient and cooperative community response.

In an innovative leap forward, we harnessed the power of geo-referencing technology to craft The Eye Tracker, a mobile solution ingeniously designed to transform the way people prepare for storms. By integrating critical data, including storm trajectories from The National Hurricane Center and cutting-edge Google technology, The Eye Tracker offers a highly personalized preparation strategy. It analyzes factors such as the storm's location and potential impact, along with the unique makeup of each household, to recommend the precise quantity of essential items needed—effectively eliminating the compulsion to overstock.

The Eye Tracker stands out not just for its practical advice but also for its groundbreaking approach to visualizing hurricane paths. It introduced the first-ever street view experience of a hurricane's projected path, enabling users to visually comprehend the impact area and prepare with the right information and supplies. This platform not only guides users in assembling a tailored list of necessary groceries but also facilitates the shopping process. Users can directly purchase these suggested items through the platform for delivery or in-store pickup, significantly alleviating the usual pre-storm frenzy and in-store chaos. This creative solution redefines disaster preparedness, merging technology with real-time data to ensure people have exactly what they need, precisely when they need it, fostering a calmer, more informed approach to facing hurricanes.
```
